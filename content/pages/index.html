Dog Agility Jump Height Calculator

Please read:
  • The calculator displays the lowest jump heights by dog height for many organizations and their main competition divisions, but not all organizations or divisions.
  • Organizations may have additional and/or optional higher or lower jump heights.
  • Organizations may have additional jump height divisions for specific breeds, junior handlers, and/or senior dogs/handlers.
  • Always confirm the rules by clicking on any organization's name!
  • Please email any corrections to steve at agilitynerd.com
